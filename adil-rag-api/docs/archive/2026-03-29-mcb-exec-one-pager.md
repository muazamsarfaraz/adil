## The Problem

**British Muslims cannot find Muslim-friendly legal help when they need it most.**

- No public, searchable directory of Muslim or Islamic-specialist solicitors exists in the UK
- Islamophobia incidents surged **377%** between 2023-2024 (IRU data)
- Muslims account for **45% of all religious hate crimes** in England & Wales
- ~12,000 Muslim solicitors practise in England & Wales — but the public has no way to find them
- Existing resources (muslimlawyer.co.uk, SolicitorConnect) are either dormant or ineffective

## What We're Asking For

### Decision 1: Approve solicitor outreach

**Action:** Authorise the AskAdil team to contact 50 solicitor firms and invite them to be listed (free of charge).

**What this involves:**
- AI-personalised outreach emails sent from noreply@mcbx.app
- Each email reviewed by a human before sending
- Firms asked to opt in — no one is listed without consent
- MCB name used: "in association with the Muslim Council of Britain"

**Risk:** Minimal. Emails are professional, personalised, and free-to-list. Worst case: firms don't respond.

**Timeline:** 6 weeks to contact all 50 firms

### Decision 2: MCB endorsement letter

**Action:** Provide a brief MCB endorsement (1 paragraph) for use in outreach emails and the directory landing page.

**Why it matters:** MCB endorsement is the #1 trust signal. Response rates will be significantly higher with it.

**Draft:**

**Action:** Share the directory with MCB's network once 25+ firms are listed.

**Channels:** MCB newsletter, social media, affiliate mosque networks

**Timeline:** After outreach phase (approximately 8-10 weeks from now)
