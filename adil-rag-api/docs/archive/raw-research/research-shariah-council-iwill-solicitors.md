## 1. MUSLIM LAW (SHARIAH) COUNCIL UK

**Website:** https://www.shariahcouncil.org/

### Organisation Overview

- **Full Name:** The Muslim Law (Shariah) Council UK
- **Established:** October 1985 (at a conference attended by 250+ Imams, Muslim scholars, and representatives of mosques/organisations)
- **Type:** Islamic advisory/dispute resolution body (NOT a parallel judiciary -- refers matters outside its remit to civil courts)
- **Track Record:** Over 3,000 cases involving Muslim family disputes investigated, deliberated, and adjudicated since inception
