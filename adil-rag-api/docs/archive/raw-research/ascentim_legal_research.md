## 4. Practice Areas / Services

### 4.1 Core Service Lines (from website navigation/service pages)

1. **Employment Law**
   - Unfair dismissal, Redundancy, Constructive dismissal
   - Race / Disability / Sex / Age discrimination
   - Settlement agreements
   - Acts for both employers and employees

2. **Immigration Law**
   - Tier 1, 2, 4 PBS applications
   - Visit visas, Spouse visas, Student visas
   - Settlement applications, Citizenship & Nationality
   - EU Law applications
   - Human Rights claims
   - Domestic Violence Rule applications
   - Long residence rules (7-year, 10-year, 20-year)
   - Appeals, Judicial Reviews, Detention & Bail
   - Sponsorship Licences for employers
   - Covers all appeal stages including First-tier Tribunal, Upper Tribunal, Court of Appeal, Supreme Court, ECHR, ECJ

3. **Wills & Probate**
   - Will writing (English law and Islamic wills)
   - Administration of estate
   - Lasting Power of Attorney
   - Litigation (contesting a will)
   - Grant of Probate
   - Inheritance tax planning

4. **Family Law**
   - Agreed & Contested Divorce
   - Annulment
   - Civil Partnership Dissolution
   - Mediation Support
   - Injunctions & Non-molestation Orders
   - Domestic Violence
   - Child custody & contact
   - Matrimonial property disputes

5. **Conveyancing**
   - Buying & selling freehold and leasehold properties
   - Transfers of equity
   - New leases & lease extensions
   - Shared ownership purchases
   - Re-mortgages
   - New build properties (acting for developers/sellers and purchasers)
   - Right to buy purchases
   - Applications for adverse possession
   - Deeds of Trusts, Deeds of Covenants
   - Advice to Landlords/Management Companies
   - Independent from any real estate agency (no referral fees)

6. **Landlord & Tenant**
   - Commercial and residential property disputes
   - **For Landlords:** Breach of tenancy, possession claims, defending unlawful eviction claims, evictions against trespassers/squatters
   - **For Tenants:** Defending possession/eviction, rent arrears claims, unlawful eviction, tenant rights, tenancy deposit disputes
   - Housing defect claims (damp, drainage, vermin, structural issues)
