## 1. FIRM IDENTITY

- **Trading Name:** Muslim Solicitors / Muslim Lawyer
- **Nature:** Not a single law firm — operates as a **network/referral platform** connecting Muslim clients with partner lawyers across London and England & Wales
- **Tagline:** "Legal help and support for Muslims in London, England and Wales"
- **USP:** Legal advice given "in accordance with the Shari'ah of Islam, while keeping you informed of all the options." They examine both Shari'ah and English law for clients' social, political and legal issues.
- **Members Area:** Has a login/register system — likely for solicitors who join their network
