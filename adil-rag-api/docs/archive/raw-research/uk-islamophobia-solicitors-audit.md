| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Website** | https://www.theiru.org.uk |
| **Type** | Independent Charity (NOT a law firm) |
| **How We Help** | https://www.theiru.org.uk/how-we-help/ |
| **Report Form** | https://www.theiru.org.uk/report-islamophobia/ |
| **Contact Page** | https://www.theiru.org.uk/contact/ |
| **Phone** | 020 3904 6555 |
| **Email** | info@theiru.org.uk |
| **Hours** | Monday - Friday, 10:00 - 17:00 (except public holidays) |
| **Coverage** | England and Wales (UK residents only) |

### What They Do
The IRU is an independent charity established to assist people subjected to Islamophobic hate crimes, incidents, and discrimination. They are NOT a law firm and do NOT provide formal legal representation or advocacy.

### Services Provided
1. **Free legal guidance** (not legal representation)
2. **Support at Employment Tribunals** (attendance, emotional support)
3. **Basic emotional support**
4. **Data collection and monitoring**
5. **Referrals to affiliates and partner organisations**
6. **Review of court applications** (guidance only)
7. **Signposting to solicitors and legal services**

### Important Limitations
- Cannot provide formal legal representation
- Cannot assist if you have already entered litigation proceedings
- Does not provide an advocacy service
- All guidance is for informational purposes only

### Impact Statistics (from January 2026 annual report)
- **377% surge** in Islamophobia incidents between 2023 and 2024
- Muslims account for **45% of all religious hate crimes** in England and Wales
- **906 incidents** investigated from 2021 to 2025
- Discrimination now accounts for **37% of all cases**
- Average employment tribunal settlements reaching **$43,234**

### Types of Cases They Assist With
- Discrimination at work or school
- Race- and religion-based bullying
- Refusals or restrictions related to prayer
- Bans or exclusions linked to Islamic clothing
- Physical assault and verbal abuse
- Harassment
- Criminal damage

### Independence
Independent of the government, police, and local authorities. Represents only client interests.

### Rating
**ESSENTIAL RESOURCE** - Not a law firm, but the primary UK charity for Islamophobia cases. Excellent first point of contact for guidance and referrals. Free service. Should be the first call for anyone experiencing Islamophobia.
