## 1. Organisation Overview

**Entity Name:** Muslim Lawyer (operating as "Muslim Lawyer UK")
**Nature:** A referral network / directory service that connects Muslim clients with partner solicitors and law firms. It is NOT itself a law firm -- it acts as an intermediary that routes enquiries to partner lawyers in their network.

**Tagline:** "Legal help and support for Muslims"
**Mission (from About page):** "We strive to connect the Muslim community together for the better. We join the service providers with the service consumers."

## 10. Assessment for AskAdil Solicitor Directory

**Usefulness: LOW.** This site does not provide any individual solicitor or firm names that could be imported into a directory. It is a competing referral service rather than a directory of named professionals. The practice area taxonomy and London area coverage list may be useful as reference for AskAdil's own categorisation scheme.

**Key takeaway:** Muslim Lawyer UK is a referral intermediary, not a directory. To find actual named Muslim solicitors, other sources (SRA register, Law Society Find a Solicitor, individual firm websites) would be more productive.
