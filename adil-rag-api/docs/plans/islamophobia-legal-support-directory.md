## 1. Why This Matters

- **377% surge** in Islamophobia incidents between 2023-2024 (IRU 2026 report)
- Muslims account for **45% of all religious hate crimes** in England & Wales
- **1,000+ Islamophobic hate crimes** recorded in London in 2025; estimated **80% go unreported**
- Average employment tribunal settlement for Islamophobia-related discrimination: **£43,234**
- Discrimination now accounts for **37% of all IRU cases**

There is no specific anti-Islamophobia law in the UK. Cases rely on:
- **Equality Act 2010** (religion/belief as protected characteristic)
- General hate crime legislation (Public Order Act 1986, Crime and Disorder Act 1998)
- **Online Safety Act 2023** and Communications Act 2003 (for online hate)
- Human Rights Act 1998

## 6. Coverage by Issue Type

### Workplace Islamophobia (Employment Discrimination)

**Common scenarios:** Hijab/niqab restrictions, prayer time refusal, Ramadan accommodation, Islamophobic bullying, name-based discrimination in hiring, dismissal linked to Muslim identity, Prevent-related workplace issues.

**Time limit:** 3 months minus 1 day from last discriminatory act to start Employment Tribunal claim. Must notify ACAS first.

| Priority | Resource |
|----------|----------|
| 1st | Report to IRU or Tell MAMA |
| 2nd | Contact ACAS (0300 123 1100) for early conciliation |
| 3rd | Instruct solicitor: Bindmans, Rahman Lowe, Landau Law (no-win-no-fee), Toner Legal (no-win-no-fee), Slater + Gordon |

### Hate Crime (Physical/Verbal Attacks)

| Priority | Resource |
|----------|----------|
| 1st | Call 999 (if in danger) or 101 (non-emergency) |
| 2nd | Report to Tell MAMA (0800 456 1226) |
| 3rd | Legal support: Saunders Law, Bhatt Murphy, Leigh Day, Hodge Jones & Allen |

### Education (School/University Discrimination)

**Common scenarios:** Hijab/uniform exclusions, Prevent referrals, teacher bullying, false allegations, exclusion from activities.

| Priority | Resource |
|----------|----------|
| 1st | Report to IRU (18.6% of their cases are education-related) |
| 2nd | Legal support: Mishcon de Reya (hijab/uniform cases), Bindmans (Prevent/faith schools), Duncan Lewis |

### Housing Discrimination

**Common scenarios:** Estate agent discrimination, landlord refusal, Muslim family profiling.

| Priority | Resource |
|----------|----------|
| 1st | Contact Shelter (0808 800 4444) |
| 2nd | Report to IRU |
| 3rd | Legal support: Hodge Jones & Allen (no-win-no-fee), Duncan Lewis (legal aid), Kesar & Co (legal aid) |

### Police / Public Services Discrimination

**Common scenarios:** Stop and search targeting, unlawful arrest, excessive force, racial/religious profiling, Prevent referrals.

| Priority | Resource |
|----------|----------|

### Geographic gaps identified
- **Outside London:** Very few Islamophobia specialist solicitors. Slater + Gordon (nationwide) and Landau Law (remote) are the main options.
- **Midlands:** Ringrose Law (East Midlands) only. No Birmingham specialist identified.
- **North:** No specialist identified for Manchester, Leeds, Bradford despite large Muslim populations.
- **Scotland / Wales:** Minimal coverage. Tell MAMA covers Wales. Scotland largely unserved.
