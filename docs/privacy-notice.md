# AskAdil Privacy Notice

**Last updated:** 2026-03-23
**Data Controller:** Muslim Council of Britain (MCB)
**Contact:** [privacy@askadil.org / MCB data protection contact]

---

## What is AskAdil?

AskAdil is a free AI legal education tool that helps you understand your rights under UK discrimination law. It is operated by the Muslim Council of Britain.

## What data we collect and why

### During conversation (all users)

| Data | Purpose | Stored? |
|------|---------|---------|
| Your messages | To provide legal guidance using AI | In your browser session only. Cleared when you close the tab. Not stored on our servers. |
| URLs you share | To extract and analyse content for legal issues | Processed in memory only. Not stored. |
| Images you upload | To analyse for potential discrimination or hate content | Processed in memory only. Not stored after analysis. |

**We do not store your conversations.** Your chat history exists only in your browser session and is lost when you close the tab or refresh the page.

### Email receipts

If you provide an email address during report submission, we will send you a confirmation email after a successful submission. This email includes your reference number, a summary of what was submitted, next steps, and useful links. It is sent from `noreply@mcbx.app` via SendGrid. We do not retain your email address after the receipt is sent.

### During police report submission (only if you choose to report)

If you click "Report to Police UK", we collect additional personal information to fill in the police hate crime reporting form on your behalf:

| Data | Purpose | Stored? |
|------|---------|---------|
| First name, surname | Required by the police reporting form | **Not stored.** Passed directly to police.uk and immediately discarded. |
| Date of birth | Required by the police reporting form | **Not stored.** Passed directly and discarded. |
| Gender | Required by the police reporting form | **Not stored.** Passed directly and discarded. |
| Email address | Required by the police reporting form (police may contact you) | **Not stored.** Passed directly and discarded. |
| Phone number (optional) | Police may contact you | **Not stored.** Passed directly and discarded. |

**How this works technically:** Your personal details pass through our servers in memory only for the few seconds it takes to submit the police form. They are never written to a database, file, or log. After submission, the data is deleted from memory. We retain only the submission timestamp and any reference number — no personal details.

## Your lawful basis

We process your data under:
- **Consent** (UK GDPR Article 6.1.a) — you explicitly confirm before any report is submitted
- **Legitimate interest** (UK GDPR Article 6.1.f) — providing legal education and helping victims of hate crime

For data relating to religion (a special category), our basis is:
- **Explicit consent** (UK GDPR Article 9.2.a) — you choose to use AskAdil and confirm before report submission
- **Substantial public interest** (UK GDPR Article 9.2.g) — supporting victims of hate crime

## Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:
- **Access** — ask what data we hold about you (answer: we don't store any)
- **Erasure** — ask us to delete your data (answer: there is nothing to delete — we don't store it)
- **Withdraw consent** — you can cancel a report submission at any time before confirming
- **Complain** — contact the ICO (ico.org.uk) if you believe your data has been mishandled

**Note:** Once a report is submitted to police.uk, we cannot recall it. The police are then the data controller for that report.

## Anonymised analytics

To improve the service, AskAdil logs **anonymised metadata** about conversations. This includes:

| What we log | Example | What we do NOT log |
|-------------|---------|-------------------|
| Topic category | "workplace_discrimination" | Your actual messages |
| Jurisdiction | "england" | Your name or location |
| Whether URLs were shared | true/false | The actual URLs |
| Whether images were uploaded | true/false | The actual images |
| Whether a report was submitted | true/false | Your personal details |
| Response time and token count | 1200ms, 500 tokens | Any conversation content |

This data is stored in a database hosted by Railway (UK/EU data centres). It cannot be linked back to you — we do not store IP addresses, session IDs, or any identifying information alongside this metadata.

**Lawful basis:** Legitimate interest (UK GDPR Article 6.1.f) — improving the service for all users.

## Data retention

| Data | Retention |
|------|-----------|

| Personal details for reports | Seconds (in memory during submission, then deleted) |
